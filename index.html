Biography

My name is Chaojie Gu. I am currently a Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research focuses on Internet of Things, Low Power Wide Area Networks. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2016.
